We know that college is an investment. As a CU Boulder undergraduate student, you will gain hands-on experience, a world-class education and the ability to think critically—all qualities that will prepare you to graduate with confidence.
Understand the value of your degree and the costs of your education early in the admission process so you can set yourself up for success.
Tuition Guarantee
The CU Boulder Tuition Guarantee offers undergraduate students four years of fixed tuition, providing you with predictable costs for your time at CU Boulder.
