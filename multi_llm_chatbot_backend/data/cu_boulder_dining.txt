Welcome to Campus Dining & Hospitality
At CU Boulder, dining is more than just a meal—it’s an experience. Our award-winning dining venues serve fresh, sustainable and innovative cuisine crafted by a passionate team dedicated to your well-being.
Every year, we proudly serve over 3 million meals, fueling healthy bodies and minds with options that are as delicious as they are nutritious. From community-building events to hands-on food education, we’re here to help Buffs build lifelong habits for a healthier future.
Menus at your fingertips
Explore what’s on the menu anytime, anywhere. With Nutrislice, you can view daily offerings at various locations, check nutritional info, filter by dietary needs and even plan your meals in advance. It’s your personalized guide to dining on campus.

Quick, self‑serve coffee
Roaming Ralphie Food Trailer (Across the street, UClub Lot 201):
Mobile dining option offering a grill‑style menu with rotating daily specials
